Frequently asked questions

  • What is a POAP NFT?
  • POAP stands for "Proof of Attendance Protocol" and an NFT is a "Non Fungible Token." A POAP NFT is a NFT that celebrates a real world event such as reading a book with a book club, similar to a training cervtificate. Think of POAP's as "Bookmarks for your Life!"

  • How do you decide which NFT books to read?
    After each book is read the community holds a vote via "Snapshot Voting" using NFT Port by using their NFT and wallet address. 
  • What if I don't like what the community chose to read?
    This is where the beauty of the POAP Book Club comes in. By joining, each member gets a "Dynamic Book NFT" via NFT Port that changes based on the outcomes of the votes. Depending on what you vote to read next, determines your next read and the community subgroup you will be a part of. After each read, the community forks to match and create group pairings with the votes so everyone gets the opportunity to read the book NFT of their choice.
  • What Blockchain is the Book NFT on?
    We accommodate all blockchains by using administrative developer tools such as TATUM and others to airdrop the Book NFT's across all Blockchains. The more important aspect is the ability to read the NFT and be a part of the community discussions. Some of the Blockchains supported or planned to be supparted are: POlygon/Matic, Optimism, ALgorand, Harmony, Stellar Lumens, Ripple, Ethereum, NEAR, Hedera, SKALE and Cronos.
  • How does the Community know when voting is happening or other events?
    POAP Book Club uses various methods to contact and ensure everyone gets informed such as sending out EPNS or EThereum Push Notification Service messages via its EPNS Channel, XMTP, Abacus, LENS Protocol, Debridge as well as non Web3 methods like Email and Discord Announcements.  
  • Do you archive the reads? How does this benefit Authors and Publishers?
    Yes, We back up all our NFT Books Using multiple IPFS servers such as FileCOin/NFT Storage, STORJ and Pinata. As well as indexing them on the blockchain using "The Graph." This Benefits Authors and publishers in multiple ways:
    -preventing censorship
    -Scaling reads and possibly even pages read compared to books distributed, allowing for more accurate sales records and data collection feedback for authors and creators to improve and understand what their audience likes, this also prevents fraud such as how the tradition publishing world might distribute many books just to get on a best seller list but none of the books may have been read, creating false statistics. Using POAP NFTs to scale who has Read the BOOK NFT to the number of BOOK NFTs distributed creates a much more accurate sales to read ratio.
    -Immutable DATA Backup, because the Books are backed up and stored using IPFS, the author never has to fear losing their hard work and creations due to something like a computer or hard drive failure.
